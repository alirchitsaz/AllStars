üìñ PYLON2PYLON RULES REFERENCE

‚ö†Ô∏è CRITICAL RULES - MEMORIZE THESE

  • 10-YARD RUSH: Rusher must start 10 yards from QB (referee marks the spot). Cannot rush until ball is snapped.
  • 5-SECOND CLOCK: QB has 5 seconds from snap to throw or hand off. Clock shown by referee. After 5 seconds = sack.
  • LINE PLAYER (RB Watch): Cannot cross line of scrimmage until pass is thrown OR handoff occurs. Most penalized rule - be patient! QB cannot run, so focus on watching the RB for handoffs.
  • NO BLOCKING: Zero contact allowed. No picks, screens, or obstruction. Pure speed and skill.
  • FLAG PULL: Two-hand touch anywhere on belt = runner is down. One-hand doesn't count.
  • NO CONTACT: Can't jam receivers at line. Must stay 1 yard away. Use footwork and speed to stay close.
  • SUBSTITUTIONS: Unlimited - coach controls all subs. Use your wristband colors!
  • QB CANNOT RUN: QB must throw or hand off. No scrambles. Line player watches RB, not QB.

üéØ QUICK REFERENCE - DEFENSIVE SCHEMES

  • 1-1-4: 1 Rusher (10yd), 1 Line (spy), 4 Coverage. Used in RED, WHITE, GREEN. Prevents big plays.
  • 2-0-4: 2 Rushers (10yd), 0 Line, 4 Coverage. Used in BLUE, YELLOW. Double pressure forces mistakes.
  • Man Coverage: Stick to your receiver. Stay between them and goal line. Break on ball.
  • Zone Coverage: Cover your area. Eyes on QB. Let receivers come to you. Break when thrown.
  • Deep Safety: 15-20 yards deep. Last line of defense. Nothing gets behind you. Help over top.
